# Verifying your identity with Singpass

Welcome to get that bread. Before you can publish a campaign and start collecting pledges, we need to confirm you are who you say you are. We do this through Singpass, the same login you already use for IRAS, CPF, and HDB. It usually takes under a minute, and once it's done, you're cleared to launch.

This page walks you through what happens, what we keep, what we don't, and how to get help if something goes sideways.

## Why we ask

Three reasons, in plain terms.

**Compliance.** Singapore takes a careful view of platforms that move money between strangers. Verifying creators is how we stay on the right side of anti-money-laundering and consumer-protection rules. No verification, no payouts.

**Backer trust.** When someone pledges, they're trusting there's a real person behind the page who will actually deliver. A "Verified Creator" badge tells them you're not a bot or a sockpuppet.

**Fraud prevention.** Verification makes it harder for bad actors to spin up fake campaigns, drain pledges, and disappear. That protects your backers and every other creator on the platform, including you.

## What we collect, and what we don't

Through Singpass, we receive a small set of fields straight from the government's records:

- Your full legal name
- Your NRIC or FIN (in a hashed form — see below)
- Your date of birth
- Your nationality
- Your residency status (citizen, PR, or pass-holder)

That's it. We do **not** receive or store:

- Your CPF balance, salary, or employment history
- Your tax filings or HDB records
- Your phone contacts, photos, or anything else on your device
- Your Singpass password (we never see it — you log in directly with Singpass)

**About your NRIC/FIN.** We never store the raw number. The moment we receive it, we run it through a one-way scrambler tied to a secret only our servers know. What lands in our database is a fingerprint that can answer "is this person already verified?" but cannot be reversed back into your NRIC.

## Step-by-step: how to verify

1. Sign in to get that bread and head to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click **Verify with Singpass**. This sends you to the official Singpass site — check the URL in your browser if you want to be sure.
3. Log in however you normally do — Singpass app, face verification, or username and password.
4. Singpass shows you exactly which fields get sent to us and asks you to consent. Review the list and tap **I Agree**.
5. You're sent back to get that bread. You'll see a confirmation screen with the details we received, so you can sanity-check them before we save anything.
6. Confirm, and you're verified. The Verified Creator badge appears on your profile and you can start drafting your campaign.

**Heads-up on timing during our May launch.** Our direct Singpass connection is in approval with GovTech, so for now we run a short manual review behind the scenes — usually within one business day, often much faster. You'll get an email the moment it clears. Once GovTech approval lands, verification flips to instant.

## Your data, your rights

We take PDPA seriously. Here's what that looks like in practice:

- **What we keep, and for how long.** Once you're verified, we keep your verification record (verified name, date of birth, nationality, residency status, timestamp, and the hashed NRIC fingerprint) for as long as your account is open, and for **seven years after you close it**. That's not us being greedy — it's MAS and anti-money-laundering rules that apply to any platform handling payouts. After that, it's gone.
- **What we use it for, and what we don't.** Verification data is used only for compliance and fraud checks. It is never used for marketing, never shared with backers or other creators, and only visible to you and to admins reviewing compliance.
- **You can ask for a copy.** Email hello@getthatbread.sg and we'll send you everything we hold on you, usually within 30 days.
- **You can ask us to delete.** If you close your account, we wipe everything we're allowed to. The pieces tied to financial and AML obligations have to stay for the legal hold period; we delete those automatically once the clock runs out.
- **We don't sell your data.** Not to advertisers, not to data brokers, not to anyone.

## Common questions

**Do I need to be a Singapore citizen?**
No. PRs and long-term pass holders with a valid FIN can verify the same way.

**Will this affect my credit score or show up anywhere?**
No. A Singpass login for identity verification is not a credit check and doesn't appear on any record outside your own Singpass activity log.

**Can I run a campaign through my company instead of myself?**
You still verify yourself first as the human running the show. Once verified, you can link a business entity and we'll do a separate light-touch check on it. Payouts can go to a personal or business PayNow.

**How often do I need to re-verify?**
Once, in most cases. If you change your legal name or residential status, ping us and we'll re-run a quick check.

**Can I delete my account after a campaign ends?**
Yes. Once your rewards are fulfilled, you can request deletion. We'll remove everything we're allowed to right away. The KYC and payout records stay locked for seven years per MAS rules, then auto-delete — there's no action needed from you.

## Trouble verifying?

If Singpass throws an error or rejects you, don't panic — it's almost always one of a few fixable things.

- **Login failures or "account not found".** Reset your Singpass password directly at singpass.gov.sg. Once you're back in, return to your dashboard and try again.
- **"Consent declined" or you tapped the wrong button.** Click **Verify with Singpass** again and walk through the consent screen.
- **The redirect spins forever or lands on an error page.** Try once more in a fresh browser tab. If it still fails, screenshot it and email us.
- **Your details on file look wrong.** Singpass pulls from official records, so corrections (old address, name spelling) happen at ICA or the relevant agency. Your next Singpass login will reflect the update.

For anything else, drop us a line at hello@getthatbread.sg. A human will write back, usually within a few hours during Singapore business hours. We want you launched and earning, not stuck on a verification screen.
